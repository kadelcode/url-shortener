Need a Help?

Create custom links

To create a custom link, follow the following steps:

  • Paste the original long URL.
  • Enter your brand domain. This field is only available for users with a paid account.
  • Enter your back half. The back half of a URL is the part of the URL that comes after the domain name. It is typically used to identify a specific page or resource on the website.
  • Click on the Create custom URL button to get your custom short link.

My Custom URLs

This section contains all the custom url you have generated. You have the access to view the list of generated custom URLs, delete them and also visit the link.

Log me out

To log out:

  • Click on the icon located in the top-right corner.
  • Then click on the Logout button
  • NB: You can also find the Logout button in the homepage.
